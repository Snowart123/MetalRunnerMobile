# Metal Runner Mobile

## Overview

Metal Runner Mobile is a 3D maze game where the player controls a 
metal ball and navigates through moving enemies and obstacles to reach the goal platform. 
This version was redesigned for mobile (mainly iOS) using touch controls and mobile-specific features.

---

## How to Play Metal Runner

- Use the virtual joystick in the bottom-left corner of the screen to move the metal ball.
- Reach the yellow goal platform to win.
- Pinch the screen with two fingers to zoom the camera in or out.
- Tilt the phone slightly to receive a small movement speed boost.

---

## Build Instructions

Platform: iOS

Minimum iOS Version: iOS 15.0

---

## Known Issues / Limitations

- Camera zoom is intentionally limited so the player cannot zoom too far into or away from the level.
- The speed boost from the accelerometer is intentionally small to keep gameplay balanced.

---

## Device Testing Notes

**Device Tested:** iPhone 15

During development, touch input felt much more natural 
on my iPhone than in the Unity Editor. 
The virtual joystick and touch gestures worked correctly on the 
iPhone after building through Xcode. Camera zoom settings were 
adjusted to prevent the camera from zooming too close into the level.
